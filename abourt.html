About Me

I was born and raised in South Central Los Angeles. Living in this environment taught me the importance of community, adaptability, and diversity. All these skills, I have brought with me into the world of design.

While growing up, I loved playing soccer. Soccer taught me the importance of team building, practice, and team work. That same passion has stayed and grown with me even to this present day; I want to build strength in teams in order to help people in my community of South Central Los Angeles.

My passion in design is heavily focused in serving the best experiences for users and helping people. I am motivated by tackling tough problems and making issues simpler through thoughtful design while also, empowering people along the way.

I hope to design for companies that have a deep appreciation of building community and have goals that I can resonate with. In essence, my goal is to craft products that empower users to enhance their lives and the world around them effortlessly and efficiently.
